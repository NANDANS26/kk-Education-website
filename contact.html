Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Basic computer knowledge and 10th grade completion. No prior programming experience needed for most courses.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement team that helps students with resume building, interview preparation, and job opportunities.

Are there flexible timing options?

Yes, we offer morning, afternoon, and evening batches. Weekend classes are also available for working professionals.

What certifications do you provide?

We provide industry-recognized certifications along with our completion certificates. Some courses include vendor certifications.

Is there an EMI option for fee payment?

Yes, we offer flexible EMI options to make quality education accessible to all students.

Do you provide online classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes. Our hybrid model allows flexibility for students.
